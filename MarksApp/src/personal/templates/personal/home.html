Instructions:

To get started, follow these simple steps!

  1. Create an account or log in if you already have one.
  2. Click "Class Search" in the top right or click here
  3. Fill in your course information: Subject, Term, and CRN using the dropdowns in "Class Search"
  4. Once you find your desired class/es, add it to your cart and/or search for another class
  5. Once you have all the classes you wish to watch, navigate to "Cart" and selected "Add to Watchlist"
  6. Now sit back and wait. Once any of your classes has a seat avaialable, we'll notify you through the email you registered with!
